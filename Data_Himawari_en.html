Light curve of Betelgeuse by the Himawari-8 meteorological satellite

  • Original Paper:
    • Daisuke Taniguchi, Kazuya Yamazaki, and Shinsuke Uno (2022)
    • Nature Astronomy, 6, 930.
    • The Great Dimming of Betelgeuse seen by the Himawari-8 meteorological satellite
    • DOI: 10.1038/s41550-022-01680-5
  • Photometric catalog for each date (csv):
    • Spectral irradiances for all the bands
    • AB magnitudes for Bands 2–3
  • Photometric catalog after binning (csv):
    • Spectral irradiances for all the bands
    • AB magnitudes for Bands 2–3
  • Figures of the light curves: black and blue dots represnt original and binned data points, respectively.
  • Only the data after January 2017 can be used. (Before that, bias was large)
  • See this page for more detail.
  • The operation of Himawari-8 finished on December 13th, 2022, and Himawari-9 is in operation now. We are analyzing and checking the Himawari-9 data.
  • The data before 2022/12/13 04:30 was taken by Himawari-8, and data after that was taken by Himawari-9. Currently, we haven't carefully validated the Himawari-9 data, so please be careful.
